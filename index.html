Build Your Career with NHS Scotland

Join Scotland's largest employer and make a real difference to people's lives. With over 300 different career paths across healthcare, we offer rewarding opportunities, excellent training, and the chance to develop your skills in a supportive environment.

Why Work With Us?

NHS Scotland is committed to providing high-quality healthcare services to communities across Scotland. As an employer, we value our staff and offer:

  • Competitive salaries and excellent pension schemes
  • Comprehensive training and professional development opportunities
  • Flexible working arrangements where possible
  • Generous annual leave entitlement that increases with service
  • Health and wellbeing support services for staff
  • Commitment to equality, diversity and inclusion

Popular Career Paths

Nursing

Provide compassionate care to patients across various specialisms including adult, children's, mental health and learning disability nursing.

Allied Health Professionals

Work in professions such as physiotherapy, occupational therapy, radiography, and dietetics to support patient recovery and wellbeing.

Healthcare Science

Apply scientific principles to prevent, diagnose and treat illnesses through roles in life sciences, physical sciences, and bioinformatics.

Administrative & Support

Keep our services running smoothly in roles including medical secretaries, porters, estates staff, and catering teams.

How To Apply

Most NHS Scotland vacancies are advertised on the NHS Scotland Jobs website. The application process typically includes:

  1. Creating an account on the NHS Scotland Jobs portal
  2. Searching for vacancies by location, profession, or keyword
  3. Completing the online application form
  4. Attending an interview and possible assessment
  5. Completing pre-employment checks including references and PVG scheme membership

For specific guidance on applications, training requirements, or career progression, use our chat assistant below.
